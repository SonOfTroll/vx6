This document describes allowed and restricted use of the "VX6" name and related branding.

"VX6" and associated logos, marks, and branding assets are project marks controlled by the project owner/licensor.

## 2. Allowed Use

You may use the name "VX6" for factual, descriptive references, including:

- stating compatibility, such as "built on VX6" or "compatible with VX6";
- identifying that your software includes or integrates VX6;
- referring to the upstream project in documentation, blogs, or technical materials.

## 3. Restricted Use

Without prior written permission, you may not:

- use "VX6" or confusingly similar marks in your product, company, or service name;
- use VX6 branding in a way that implies sponsorship, endorsement, certification, or official partnership;
- use project logos/marks as your primary brand identity for commercial offerings.

## 4. Apache-2.0 Consistency

This policy is intended to be consistent with Apache-2.0 Section 6 (Trademarks) and does not expand Apache license rights.
